About Me

  • My Story

    I began my creative career in the First Grade, when I won a local coloring contest. Ever since, I've been creating illustrations and designs using everything from watercolors and colored pencils to Adobe Creative Suite and Sketch.

    After graduating from Southwestern University in Communications, I worked in Creative Staffing where I helped Lead Designers, Art Directors and Creative Teams find Digital Creative talent. I learned all about the field of UI/UX and took classes at the Art Institute of Austin and General Assembly and in 2017, I completed an immersive UI Design program.

    Now my passion for art and drawing, combined with my experience in the digital creative industry, give me the unique ability to create informed and functional designs.

    When I'm not sketching on my ipad or watching Bob's Burgers, you'll find me running at LadyBird lake, swimming laps at Deep Eddy, or sipping on a fromar (frozen margarita) at Matt's El Rancho.
